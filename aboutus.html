About Us

Your Trusted Cybersecurity Partner

Who We Are

Web Box Pentester (Wbox Pentest) is a penetration testing organization specializing in ethical hacking and cybersecurity. We identify website vulnerabilities through passive, lawful scanning and notify the owners — privately, responsibly, and without public exposure.

Unlike public vulnerability platforms, we do not publish or sell your data. Our goal is simple: help website owners understand their risks and fix them before attackers strike.

What We Do

Passive Scanning

We analyze publicly available data without interfering with normal website traffic or user experience. No active exploitation. No damage.

Private Notification

When a potential vulnerability is detected, we contact the website owner directly with a detailed report — including vulnerability type, location, and potential impact.

Security Services (Paid)

If requested, we provide: Full penetration testing, Configuration review, Patch management guidance, Security training.

Our Model

Web Box Pentester primarily operates as a paid security service provider.

(When volunteer resources are occasionally available, limited free notifications may be offered — but this is not guaranteed.)

We do not act like Shodan or any public scanner. We inform only the website owner — never the public.

What We NEVER Do

Publicly disclose your vulnerabilities
Damage or disrupt your website
Sell your data or scan results
Publish anything without permission

We follow CFAA, GDPR, and international cybersecurity laws.

Why We Started

We observed that many website owners remain unaware of security flaws in their systems. Rather than publicly exposing them, we adopted a responsible disclosure policy — combined with paid professional services for those who need deeper help.
